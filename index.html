    Are you a Master Mind?

    The original Master Mind is essentially a code-breaking game for two players (here one is the computer).

    The game selects some random colors (choose how many to start new game).
    Your goal is to guess what the game randomly chose!

    How?

    Before you there are 4 empty slots, click on them to choose your color combination, then submit with the button on the right.

    The computer gives you an answer trough 4 smaller dots beside your previous guess:
    • Red dot(s): you positioned well that many colors
    • White dot(s): you chose well that many colors (but in the wrong position)
    Example:
    • If you get 4 white dots that means that you only have to rearrange the colors of your last guess.
    • If you get 4 red dots... you won! You're a Mastermind!
